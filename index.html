About Company


We are group of highly experienced professionals with hands on exposure in the area of clearing and Freight Forwarding for over 15 years. Over the years we have developed a very selective client list whose interests have been served by us to their fullest satisfaction.


SISA Global is a entity which binds together on a common platform of excellence, be it any sphere of life. Entrusted with creating a model unique from various methodologies of doing business, effort has been to ensure customer satisfaction based on highest professional quality standards. To our team members we promise not the world but a family which will reciprocate their efforts not only in terms of business but towards a long lasting harmonious association..


Our Presence


INFORMATION: SISA Global has offices in New Delhi, Jaipur, Panipat and Mumbai. Through these offices we cater to the Northern and Western India exporter communities requirement of end to end services from various Inland Container Depots (ICD) for FCL and Container Freight Station (CFS) for LCL cargo. Though the company is young we already have our own offices in Guangzhou, China and Philadelphia in USA (addresses and contact details are attached) . We plan to have offices or representations in major business areas in India along with representation in Nepal , Bangladesh and Sri Lanka..
